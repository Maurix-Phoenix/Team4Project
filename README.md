- **Team Size:** 6<br />
- **Duration:** June 2023 - September 2023<br />
- **Platform:** PC - Web Browser <br />

- If you want to play on Web Browser click here to play on itch.io.<br>

<h1>The game</h1>

<dl>
 <dd>
  <p align="center">
   
  <img alt="UI_MainMenu" width="200" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Maurix-Phoenix/Team4Project/main/Gif/Curses_UI_MainMenu.gif">
  <img alt="UI_LevelSelect" width="200" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Maurix-Phoenix/Team4Project/main/Gif/Curses_UI_LevelSelect.gif">
  <img alt="UI_GamePlay" width="200" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Maurix-Phoenix/Team4Project/main/Gif/Curses_UI_GamePlay.gif">
  </p>
  
  Curses! is a 3D horizontal scrolling hyper casual game in which the player is in charge of a ship in a piratical world.<br />
  He has to travel the seas facing battles against other ships, kill sea monsters and destroy defensive walls in order to reach the ending beach.<br/>
  The game is structured in different levels, and the game has a ranking system that evaluates the completion of every level.<br/>
  In every level the player can obtain 3 skulls and can unlock a Flag.<br />

 </dd>
</dl>

# <h3>The Player</h3>

<dl>
 <dd>
  
  To achieve the final goal, the Player's Ship is equipped with two essential abilities: *Vertical Movement* and *Shooting Ability*.<br/>

  |**Vertical Movement**|**Shooting Ability**|
  |:---:|:---:|
  |<img alt="Player_Movement" width="200" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Maurix-Phoenix/Team4Project/main/Gif/Curses_Player_Movement.gif">|<img alt="Player_Shoot1" width="300" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Maurix-Phoenix/Team4Project/main/Gif/Curses_Player_Shoot1.gif"> <br /> <img align="center" alt="Player_Shoot2" width="300" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Maurix-Phoenix/Team4Project/main/Gif/Curses_Player_Shoot2.gif">| 
  |Press `W` or `↑` to move the ship to a UPPER lane.<br />Press `S` or `↓` to to move the ship to a LOWER lane.<br />Player can move in 3 lane.|Press `Spacebar` to SHOOT a cannonball from the ship.|
 
 </dd>
</dl>
